Purpose and Mission

Our inspirational purpose and mission—to connect, protect, explore and inspire the world through aerospace innovation—reminds us of the significance of what we do and the effect it has on the world.

Connect

We connect people, products and ideas like never before.

Protect

We protect people and nations, and maintain global peace and stability.

Explore

We explore the mysteries of the universe, from outer space to the depths of the ocean.

Inspire

We inspire future generations of innovators, explorers, dreamers and doers.

Aspiration

As we look to the future, we must be more bold and visionary than ever before to compete and win big in our markets, deliver superior value to our customers and partners, and achieve our goals. That’s why our aspiration is nothing less than to be clearly recognized by all stakeholders and measures as the best in aerospace and an enduring global industrial champion—a top performer in each and every area of our business.

It’s a very high bar, and it’ll take our combined focus and teamwork to achieve it.

Enterprise Strategy

One of the keys to building a bigger, better Boeing is clearly defining for ourselves the top-level strategies that underpin our work, shape our daily actions, and provide a guidepost for our business units and functions.

That’s why our Enterprise Strategy contains 3 simple, easy-to-remember elements:
  • 1
    Operate as One Boeing
  • 2
    Build Strength on Strength
  • 3
    Sharpen and Accelerate to Win

The first part, Operate as One Boeing, demands that we work together and share the resources, skills and experiences embedded across the enterprise to deliver the best solutions to our customers. When we work together as a One Boeing team, we’re tough to beat.

  • Boeing-wide mindset / decisions
  • Enterprise strategy and enablers
  • Talent, resources and know-how
  • Personal accountability /shared responsibility
  • Global, collaborative leadership

With Build Strength on Strength, the second component of our strategy, we’ll succeed in an increasingly challenging environment by driving our business unit and functional strategies, leadership foundation and the things we already do well such as disciplined innovation and lifecycle solutions. This will allow us to meet our commitments, better serve our customers, and achieve business and functional objectives.

  • Integrated business strategies
  • Functional excellence
  • Core competencies
  • Leadership foundation
  • Customer commitments

The third component of our Enterprise Strategy, Sharpen and Accelerate to Win, will help us achieve step-change improvement in areas such as best team and talent, innovation and growth, and performance and productivity. Put simply, our goals require us to clear a higher bar.

  • Step-change improvement
  • Best team and talent
  • Innovation and growth
  • Performance and productivity
  • Full potential realized
Together, our three-part Enterprise Strategy, supported by and integrated with business unit and functional strategies, will position us for—and enable—ongoing success.

2025 Goals

To be the best in aerospace and an enduring global industrial champion by 2025, we strive to be characterized and measured by:

  • Market Leadership: commercial, defense and services
  • Consistent Top-Quartile Performance and Returns
  • Growth Fueled by Productivity
  • Design, Manufacturing, Services Excellence
  • Accelerated Innovation
  • Global Scale and Depth
  • Best Team, Talent and Leaders
  • Top Corporate Citizen

Enduring Values

We’re committed to a set of core values and principles that not only define who we are, but also serve as guideposts to help us become the company we’d like to be. We all must aspire to live these values every day.

Boeing Behaviors

Grounded in our Enduring Values, the Boeing Behaviors describe the approach we take to our work as we strive to achieve our 2025 goals. The Boeing Behaviors hold us accountable to a unifying set of expectations for how we work.

Business Imperative

As a business operating in a highly competitive global industry, we must balance the interests of and deliver superior value to our customers, employees, shareholders, communities and partners. It’s only through sustained top performance across these key areas that we’ll differentiate ourselves from our competitors and remain relevant in the aerospace industry.
